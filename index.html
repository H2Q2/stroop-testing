Stroop Effect Test

Welcome to this experiment. In this test, words will appear on the screen one by one. Each word will be printed in a specific color (Red, Green, or Blue).

Your task is to identify the COLOR of the ink that the word is printed in, while IGNORING the meaning of the word itself.

Please respond as quickly and accurately as possible using the following keys on your keyboard:

R for RED ink G for GREEN ink B for BLUE ink

For example, if you see the word "GREEN" printed in RED ink, you should press the R key.

We will start with a short practice block (6 trials) to help you get used to the task, followed by the formal experiment.
